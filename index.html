Virtual Office

View the Virtual Office demo for Google Cardboard
  1. Download the YouTube App
  2. Come back to this page at cufusion.net
  3. Click on the VIRTUAL OFFICE link. This will launch YouTube
  4. Place in VR mode by tapping on the ‘VR Glass’ icon
  5. Open the top flap of your Google Cardboard Glasses
  6. Insert your phone
  7. Enjoy the VR Experience
